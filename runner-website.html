Controls

How To Play

Two moves. Infinite combinations.

↑

Jump

Press Space, Arrow Up, or W to jump. You get a double jump — use it wisely to clear tall obstacles or recover mid-air.

↓

Duck

Press Arrow Down or S to duck. Required to dodge lasers and missiles flying at head height. Hold to stay low.

📱

Tap (Mobile)

Tap anywhere to jump. Works on touch screens — double-tap for a double jump.

Under the Hood

Built Different

01

Procedural Difficulty

Obstacle frequency and variety scale dynamically with your score. New threat types unlock progressively so the challenge always feels earned, never cheap.

02

Inverted Zen Mode

The only game mode where death is the objective. Obstacles glow teal and beckon you — dodge them and lose a life. A combo system rewards consecutive hits.

03

Per-Mode Theming

Every mode has its own color palette, background atmosphere, and visual effects. Nightmare bleeds red, Zen pulses teal, Time Attack pulses orange as time runs low.

04

Pixel-Perfect Collision

Collision detection uses tight inner hitboxes so near-misses feel thrilling rather than unfair. Spikes, lasers, missiles, walls and bouncing enemies each have unique behavior.

05

Zero Dependencies

Pure HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools, no loading screens. Open it and play instantly on any device with a browser.

06

Persistent Best Scores

Best scores are tracked per mode within your session. Beat your own records across all four modes in a single sitting.
